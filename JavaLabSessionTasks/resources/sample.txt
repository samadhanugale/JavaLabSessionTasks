What is the tree?
Image result for tree
Trees are all plants and carry out the life processes that all plants share. ... 
Trees may be cone-bearing plants (gymnosperms), flowering plants (angiosperms) or ferns.
 All the groups of plants that include trees are vascular plants. 
This means they have vascular tissues called xylem and phloem.
